Kyria

Kyria is a split keyboard designed by Thomas Baart. 

Availability

The Kyria is closed source, and available at splitkb.com and Little Keyboards.

Layout

A KLE is available here. The outer columns can be snapped off for a 10u board rather than a 12u board. 

Case Options

  • Little Keyboards Cases
  • Splitkb.com Acrylic Plate Case
  • Kyria Acrylic High Profile Case
  • 3DP Case
