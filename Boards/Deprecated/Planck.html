Planck

Planck is a unibody ortho keyboard designed by OLKB.

Availability

Planck is a closed-source board, available at OLKB.

Layout

The most common layout used by Planck is the MIT layout. The alternate is a full 1u grid layout.

Case Options

  • OLKB Case and PCB
  • Drop Case and PCB
  • ZSA Case and PCB
