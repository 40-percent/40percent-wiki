QAZ

QAZ is a keyboard, designed by tominabox1 and whydobearsxplod. It is row staggered, and pro micro based.

Availability

QAZ is closed source, and is available at Coffee Break Keyboards.

Layout

A KLE is available here.

Case Options

There are a variety of third party case options. This list is incomplete and additions are welcome.

  • P3D Acrylic Case
  • P3D 3D Printed Case
  • QAZKET
  • QAZ-Simple (BriefQAZ is this but with a handle)
  • QAZ-Tilt
