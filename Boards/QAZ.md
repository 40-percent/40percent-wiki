# QAZ

QAZ is a keyboard, designed by tominabox1 and whydobearsxplod. It is row staggered, and pro micro based. An integrated version is now available as well.

![qaz.jpeg](/qaz.jpeg){.align-right}

### Availability

QAZ is closed source, and is available at [Coffee Break Keyboards](https://www.cbkbd.com/product/qaz-keyboard-kit).
